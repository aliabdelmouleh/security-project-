About Savini

Our website is dedicated to help users check the strength of their passwords, generate random strong passwords and securily save them in our servers out of the reach of bad people.

This is our story.

Our journey began with a simple goal: to enhance online security. Driven by innovation, we created a platform offering password strength checking, random password generation, and secure storage. Our story is one of dedication to user privacy and convenience, shaping the future of digital security.

Why we created Savini

We developed this multifaceted website to empower users with comprehensive password management solutions, including robust features such as password strength checking, random password generation, and secure password storage. Our goal is to enhance online security and simplify password management, ensuring users can navigate the digital landscape with confidence and peace of mind.
